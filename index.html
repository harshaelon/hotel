About Villa Serinity LeSejour

Villa Serinity LeSejour One of its kind!

Villa Serenity Le Sejour Pondicherry is a French Style Villa located on the Serenity Beach Road, East Coast Road (ECR) Kottakuppam, 3kms from Pondicherry White town, Sri Aurobindo Ashram, 3 kms from Auroville and 1.5Km from Pondicherry airport. The villa has 5 executive AC bedrooms with attached bath rooms, geyser, a flat-screen LED TV, kitchen equipped with a gas stove, microwave oven, fridge, washing machine and a play area. At the basement area an open hall with 1200 Square feet granite flooring is available to host Birthday party, Anniversary party, New Year party, Corporate Annual Board Meeting, Reunion etc. Games such as Table tennis, Carom, Chess are available for free.
